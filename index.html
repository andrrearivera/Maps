Exploring Guatemala

Guatemala, known as "The Land of Eternal Spring," is a small country in Central America. Guatemala is full of rich culture and ancient architecture and is known worldwide as the heart of the Mayan world. Guatemala has various landscapes, from black-sand beaches and rocky mountain ranges to lush jungles and modern cities, perfect for any adventure. This beautiful country contains something for everyone to enjoy.

In the map above, you will find 20 different locations, perfect for tourists to gain a proper understanding of the country and its people. Click on the markers to explore some of what Guatemala has to offer.
